## 第1部：開返個 GCKey，如果你已經有 GCKey 就直接去第2部

### <font size=4>步驟 1 : 打開瀏覽器輸入 [https://www.canada.ca/](https://www.canada.ca/), 去到加拿大移民局嘅網站</font>

![步驟 1](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/1-1.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 2 : 選擇 <b>Immigration and citizenship</b></font>

![步驟 2](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/1-2.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 3 : 再選擇 <b>My application</b></font>

![步驟 3](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/1-3.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 4 : 選擇 <b>Sign in or create an account</b> 登入你嘅帳號</font>

![步驟 4](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/1-4.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 5 : 如果你係第一次使用，而又無 GCKey 就要選擇 <b>Register</b> 入去申請，一定要記住你設定的提示，因為每次登入都會問你</font>

![步驟 5](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/1-5.png)

## 第2部：登入系統

### <font size=4>步驟 1 : 有 GCKey 就直接選擇 <b>Sign in with GCKey</b></font>

![步驟 1](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/2-1.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 2 : 登入嘅畫面，輸入你的 <b>Username</b> 同 <b>Password</b> 登入</font>

![步驟 2](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/2-2.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 3 : 登入之後選擇 <b>Continue</b></font>

![步驟 3](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/2-3.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 4 : 如確認 <b>Terms and Conditions</b> 就可以選擇 <b>I Accept</b></font>

![步驟 4](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/2-4.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 5 : 系統每次登入都要認證你嘅身份，輸入之前設定的問題答案就選擇 <b>Continue</b></font>

![步驟 5](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/2-5.png)

## 第3部：開始填表

### <font size=4>步驟 1 : 選擇 <b>Apply to come to Canada</b></font>

![步驟 1](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-1.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 2 : 選擇 <b>Visitor visa, study and/or work permit</b></font>

![步驟 2](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-2.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 3 : 問卷部分，<b>What would you like to do in Canada?</b> 選擇 <b>Work</b></font>

![步驟 3](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-3.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 4 : <b>How long are you planning to stay in Canada?</b> 選擇 <b>Temporarliy - more than 6 months</b></font>

![步驟 4](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-4.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 5 : <b>Select the code that matches the one on your passport</b> 選擇你會用邊本戶照去申請，如果你用<b>特區戶照</b>去申請就選擇 <b>CHN (China (Hong Kong SAR))</b></font>

![步驟 5](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-5.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 6 : 如果你用<b>BNO</b>去申請就選擇 <b>GBN (UK - British subject)</b></font>

![步驟 6](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-6.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 7 : <b>What is your current country/territory of residence?</b> 如果你現在身處香港就選擇 <b>Hong Kong SAR</b>，如果你現在身處加拿大就選擇 <b>Canada</b></font>

![步驟 7](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-7.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 8 : <b>Do you have a family member who is a Canadian citizen or permanent resident and is 18 years or older?</b> 如實作答便可</font>

![步驟 8](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-8.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 9 : <b>What is your date of birth?</b> 選擇你的出生年月日，再選擇 <b>Continue</b></font>

![步驟 9](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-9.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 10 : <b>Are you a lawful permanent resident of the United States with a valid U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) number?</b> 選擇 <b>No</b></font>

![步驟 10](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-10.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 11 : 如果無 LIMA 就選擇 <b>No</b></font>

![步驟 11](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-11.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 12 : 你有無打算做以下工作？如果有打算就選擇 <b>Yes</b>，如果無打算就選擇 <b>No</b></font>

![步驟 12](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-12.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 13 : <b>Will you be coming to Canada under an active public policy or special measure announced by IRCC?</b> 選擇 <b>Yes</b></font>

![步驟 13](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-13.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 14 : 你有無伴侶或小朋友申請緊或申請工簽或學簽，如果有就選擇 <b>Yes</b>，無就選擇 <b>No</b></font>

![步驟 14](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-14.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 15 : 如你有無係加拿大畢業，如果有就選擇 <b>Yes</b>，無就選擇 <b>No*</font>

![步驟 15](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-15.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 16 : 婚姻狀況</font>

![步驟 16](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-16.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 17 : 選擇你想去的省，不影響你實際去的省</font>

![步驟 17](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-17.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 18 : <b>注意</b>如果無出現 <b>Open work permit (Outside Canda)</b> 好大可能之前有野選擇錯誤，請重新選擇</font>

![步驟 18](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-18.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 19 : 選擇 <b>Continue</b></font>

![步驟 19](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-19.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 20 : <b>What type of work permit do you want to apply for?</b> 選擇 <b>A work permit under an active public policy or special measure announced by IRCC</b></font>

![步驟 20](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-20.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 21 : 如果屋企人最近獲批准去加拿大就選擇 <b>Yes</b></font>

![步驟 21](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-21.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 22 : 如實作答便可</font>

![步驟 22](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-22.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 23 : 12個月內有無做過體檢，如實作答便可</font>

![步驟 23](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-23.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 24 : <b>Have you visited or lived in any one of the designated countries for 6 consecutive months in the last year?</b> 選擇 <b>Yes</b></font>

![步驟 24](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-24.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 25 : 選擇 <b>Next</b></font>

![步驟 25](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-25.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 26 : 如果同屋企人一齊申請去加拿大記得選擇 <b>Yes</b>，如果唔係，就選擇 <b>No</b></font>

![步驟 26](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-26.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 27 : 選擇 <b>No</b></font>

![步驟 27](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-27.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 28 : 選擇 <b>Yes</b></font>

![步驟 28](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-28.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 29 : 選擇 <b>Yes</b>，可以用手機影相上傳，但檔案唔可以大過 <b>4MB</b></font>

![步驟 29](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-29.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 30 : 選擇 <b>Yes</b></font>

![步驟 30](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-30.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 31 : <b>Review your answers</b> 小心檢查，選擇 <b>Continue</b></font>

![步驟 31](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-31.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 32 : 選擇 <b>Continue</b></font>

![步驟 32](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-32.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 33 : 下面4個地方，可以上傳Not Appliable的文件檔</font>

![步驟 33](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-33.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 34 : 注意檔案要細過 <b>4MB</b>，像素最少 <b>96 DPI</b></font>

![步驟 34](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-34.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 35 : <b>Proof that you Meet the Requirements of the Job Being Offered (required)</b> 上傳你5年內畢業嘅證書，成績表及其他證明</font>

![步驟 35](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-35.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 36 : 選擇 <b>Application for Work Permit Made Outside of Canada (IMM1295) (required)</b></font>

![步驟 36](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-36.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 37 : 然後下載到電腦打開</font>

![步驟 37](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/3-37.png)

## 第4部：填寫 PDF 檔案

### <font size=4>步驟 1 : 入到 PDF 檔案，選擇 <b>Validate</b> 見到紅色就一定要填的資料</font>

![步驟 1](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/4-1.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 2 : <b>DETAILS OF INTENDED WORK IN CANADA</b> - <b>What type of work permit are you applying for?</b> 選擇 <b>Open Work Permit</b></font>

![步驟 2](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/4-2.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 3 : <b>My occupation in Canada will be:</b> - <b>Job Title</b> 填 <b>HKPPTR</b>，<b>Description of duties</b> 填 <b>Hong Kong public policy - open work permit</b></font>

![步驟 3](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/4-3.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 4 : <b>18歲以下</b>先要簽名，填完資料之後選擇 <b>Validate</b></font>

![步驟 4](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/4-4.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 5 : 成功的話會多咗一頁Barcode出現，儲存檔案便可</font>

![步驟 5](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/4-5.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 6 : IMM5707E PDF 檔案，填寫伴侶/屋企人的資料，如果無伴侶簽名便可</font>

![步驟 6](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/4-6.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 7 : 填寫子女的資料，如果無子女簽名便可</font>

![步驟 7](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/4-7.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 8 : 最後簽名確認</font>

![步驟 8](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/4-8.png)

### <font size=4>步驟 9 : 確認交齊資料。就可以選擇 <b>Next</b> 俾錢，最後簽名確認</font>

![步驟 9](/public/images/2021-02-08-owp-how-to-apply/4-9.png)
